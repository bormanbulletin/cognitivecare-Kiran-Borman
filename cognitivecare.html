How it works
MIT's Tsai Lab found that 40Hz light flicker entrains gamma brainwaves, activating microglia to clear amyloid-beta plaques linked to Alzheimer's. Early human trials (GENUS study) showed reduced brain atrophy and some cognitive stabilization over 3–6 months of daily use.
Tip: Dim the room, sit comfortably, and focus gently on the screen. Do not use if you have photosensitive epilepsy. Pair with 40Hz binaural audio (search YouTube) for enhanced effect.
